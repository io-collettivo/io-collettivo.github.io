NAMA STUDIOS becomes IO & YOU

Welcome to IO&YOU, the signature chill room experience brought to events by the IO Multimedia collective. This installation is designed as an interactive space exploring sound, visuals, and hidden energies. Discover the different facets of the experience:

  • Dub Station: An interactable launchpad to play with the background music.
  • Visuals: Live capture of an interactive liquid light show.
  • EMF Recorder: Explore electromagnetic fields in a dedicated space.

Visuals

The visual heart of IO&YOU is a captivating live liquid light show. At the center of the room, a bowl filled with coloured liquids provides the source for the projections you see. You are invited to interact directly and gently with the liquids.

  • Observe the live projection capturing the liquid dynamics.
  • Approach the central bowl and gently interact with the coloured liquids.
  • Notice how your actions directly influence the projected visuals in real-time.

EMF Recorder

Explore the unseen world with our EMF Recorder, a great tool for sensing electromagnetic fields. For this installation, we are utilizing the vocal booth of the host studio. Inside, you can experiment with the recorder near various electronic devices provided.

Step inside the vocal booth to experience the EMF Recorder.

Use the device near the different electronic items available.

Observe the resulting audio and visual feedback representing the detected electromagnetic fields.
