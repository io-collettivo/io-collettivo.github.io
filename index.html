IO&YOU Installation Summary

Welcome to the IO&YOU multimedia installation, a vibrant exploration of sound, visuals, and interaction. This exhibit features several key components, each offering a unique facet of the IO&YOU experience.

  • Dub Station: An interactive sound manipulation environment.
  • Visuals: Dynamic and responsive visual projections.
  • EMF Recorder: An exploration of electromagnetic fields through sensory data.
  • Who We Are: Information about the creators and collaborators behind IO&YOU.
  • Work With Us: Opportunities for engagement and collaboration.

Navigate through the sections above to delve deeper into each aspect of the installation.
