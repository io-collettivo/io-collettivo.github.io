Ableton Production

In-depth course on music production using Ableton Live. START MAY 2025 at Nuovo Anfiteatro Martesana. 2ND Phase after summer break

Course Highlights

Unlock Your Creativity

Dive deep into Ableton Live's powerful tools. Learn synthesis, sampling, mixing, and mastering techniques to bring your musical ideas to life. This course covers everything from basic interface navigation to advanced production workflows.

Hands-On Learning

Engage in practical exercises and projects designed to solidify your understanding. Get personalized feedback from experienced instructors and collaborate with fellow students. Build a portfolio piece throughout the course.

Industry Insights

Gain valuable insights into the music industry landscape. Learn about arrangement, sound design trends, and strategies for finishing and releasing your tracks. Prepare yourself for the next step in your musical journey.
